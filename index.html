📜 How to Play

🎯 Goal

Surround enemy dots with yours to capture territory. Most captures wins!

⚡ Gameplay

  • 🔴 Red Dots and 🟢 Green Pix take turns placing dots on grid intersections
  • When your dots form a closed loop — enemy dots inside are captured
  • Connection lines appear only after an actual capture
  • Captured dots are marked with a cross and score points for you

🌐 Online

  • Create a room → share the 6-digit code with your friend
  • Friend enters code → battle begins instantly!

⚔️ AI Difficulty

  • Rookie — random, perfect for kids
  • Warrior — captures territory, blocks your moves
  • Legend — attacks, defends, full minimax analysis
