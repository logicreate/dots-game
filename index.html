📜 DOTS WAR — How to Play

🎯 Goal

Surround enemy dots with yours to capture territory. Most captures wins!

⚡ Gameplay

  • 🔴 Red and 🟢 Green take turns placing dots on grid intersections
  • Closed loop around enemy dots = captured!
  • Connection lines appear only after an actual capture
  • Captured dots score points for you

🌐 Online

  • Create a room → share the 6-digit code
  • Friend enters code → battle begins!

⚔️ AI Levels

  • 🟢 Rookie — random, great for kids
  • 🔵 Warrior — captures and blocks, 1-move lookahead
  • 🔴 Legend — attacks + defends, full 3-ply minimax
